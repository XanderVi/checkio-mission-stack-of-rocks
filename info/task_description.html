You have a few rocks with masses m1, m2, ..., mn. Your task is to divide this rocks into 2 stacks in such way that the mass difference between 2 stacks will be minimal. And then return the difference.

Input: List of the rockses masses.

Output: The minimal mass difference.

How it is used: For sorting different objects in the optimal way (for shipping, storaging etc.).

Precondition:
2 <= the amount of the rocks <= 100
1 <= mass of the rock <= 100 000
